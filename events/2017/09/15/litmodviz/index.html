Literary Modeling and Visualization Lab

Published: Sep 15, 2017

The Literary Modeling and Visualization Lab meets weekly to discuss recent scholarship in the field.

To all those interested in formal and computational approaches to the study of literature,

The Literary Modeling and Visualization Lab will hold its first meeting this Friday, 4-5pm at Butler 208b. We will be discussing:

Long, Hoyt, and Richard Jean So. “Turbulent Flow A Computational Model of World Literature.” Modern Language Quarterly 77, no. 3 (September 1, 2016): 345–67.

Please bring a print-out of the text.
