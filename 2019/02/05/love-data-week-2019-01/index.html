Love Data Week 2019

Published: Feb 5, 2019

Show your love for data and the Libraries during Love Data Week

Get involved in this week’s Postcard Project, Love Data Week 2019 Instagram Challenge, Love Data Photobooths, and, finally, Love Data Kisses!

February 11–15 is not only Columbia’s annual week of Wellness Days activities, but it also marks Love Data Week. At the Studio, we support both wellness and loving data, and we are teaming up with our colleagues in the Columbia University Libraries to provide some activities that generate public data in the interest of this year’s Love Data Week theme, “data in everyday life.”

Postcard Project

The Libraries are letting you send postcards to your loved ones around the world this week. At nine Columbia Libraries (Avery Burke, Butler, Journalism, Lehman, Science and Engineering, Social Work, and Watson), you will be able to fill out postcards, and Columbia Libraries will pick up the postage. In exchange, we’ll be keeping track of what states and countries the postcards are addressed to, and we’re building a world map of places our community is reaching out to this week:

Love Data Week 2019 Instagram Challenge

Similarly, the Science, Engineering, and Social Sciences Libraries Division (SESSL) is organizing an Instagram Challenge. Throughout their five libraries (Journalism, Lehman, Science and Engineering, Social Work, and Watson), they have placed large Valentines. Your goal is to photograph yourself in front of one and then post it to Instagram, hashtagged #LoveData19 and #LoveColumbia. We’ll give out gift cards to three lucky winners: first, best, and “other.”

At the end of the week, we’ll build a data visualization of when the competing stories were posted.

Love Data Photobooths

SESSL is also organizing an opportunity to have you pose in their photobooths to announce your support of open data and the Libraries. One booth will be in Lehman all week. The other one will be roaming around the other SESSL Libraries, Burke, and Butler. Both will be at Butler in on Thursday evening during the Night @ Butler event, from 7:00pm to 9:00pm.

Full schedule for roaming photobooth:

  • Monday: Burke, Science and Engineering, Social Work
  • Tuesday: Burke, Science and Engineering, Social Work
  • Wednesday: Burke, Journalism, Watson
  • Thursday: Burke, Butler, Journalism, Watson

Love Data Kisses

Finally, there are chocolate kisses floating around this week from the Libraries and from the open data community. Find them and enjoy them, either by sharing them with loved ones or keeping them all to yourself. Or something in between.
