Studio at Lehman

Image by: Moacir P. de Sá Pereira

Spring 2019 Open Labs (Jan 22 – May 6)
  • Mondays – Thursdays, 12:00 pm – 4:00 pm

Studio at Lehman offers space for about 25 participants in a room within Lehman Social Sciences Library in the International Affairs Building. During Open Labs, Studio at Lehman also hosts walk-in consultations with members of the Research Data Services staff as well as Digital Center Interns. RDS maintains a schedule for walk-ins and provides guidance with geographic information systems, numerical analysis, data management, data visualization, and web development.

Additionally, Studio at Lehman has six Dell Precision Tower 5810 PCs with 32GB of RAM and 30-inch (2560x1600) monitors running Windows 10. The machines are outfitted with a wide array of Studio–supported software, including:

Anaconda. Anaconda is the fastest and easiest way to do Python and R data science and machine learning on Linux, Windows, and Mac OS X.

Atom. A hackable text editor for the 21st Century.

Git. Git is a free and open source distributed version control system designed to handle everything from small to very large projects with speed and efficiency.

MiKTeX. MiKTeX is an up-to-date implementation of TeX/LaTeX, a typesetting system intended for the creation of beautiful books.

Pandoc. A universal document converter.

QGIS. Create, edit, visualize, analyze and publish geospatial information on Windows, Mac, and Linux with QGIS.

RStudio Desktop. RStudio is an integrated development environment (IDE) for R.

Zotero. Zotero is a free, easy-to-use tool to help you collect, organize, cite, and share research.

The computers sit in three banks of two, facing a screen with a projector. A cluster of movable tables creates a large, central table in the middle.

Getting to Studio at Lehman

  1. Take the ❶ to 116th St. Columbia University.
  2. Cut across campus to Amsterdam Ave.
  3. Cross Amsterdam, turn left, and turn right on 118th.
  4. Enter the International Affairs Building at 420 118th.
  5. Descend the stairs. Alternatively, take the left-hand elevators on entering IAB to the third floor. Enter Lehman Social Sciences Library on the third floor.
  6. After entering through the turnstile cut back across the front desk and follow the signs that read “DSSC” to a stairway among the DSSC computers. Alternatively, ask at the front desk for elevator access to the second floor.
  7. Studio at Lehman is at the bottom of the stairs, room 215.
